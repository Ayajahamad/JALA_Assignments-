Box 5

This box has auto margin on the left and right to center it horizontally and padding of 15px.

The examples above illustrate the difference between padding and margin:

  • Padding: The space inside the element, between the element's content and its border.
  • Margin: The space outside the element, between the element's border and the next adjacent element.
